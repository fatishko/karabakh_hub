**Karabakh Hub** is a comprehensive digital bridge designed for the "Smart Village" reconstruction projects in Karabakh. Developed during the **"Green Karabakh" Hackathon 2025**, this project secured **2nd Place** for its innovative approach to social and economic integration.

While infrastructure (solar panels, smart grids) is vital, a village is defined by its people. Our mission is to bridge the gap between high-tech hardware and human connection. We aim to build a **Resilient Community** and provide **Direct Economic Support** to the residents of Karabakh's new smart villages.

The platform provides a tailored experience for two distinct user groups:

* **Community Board:** A dedicated space for neighbors to help each other, share announcements, and organize local events.

* **Smart Tech Guide:** Educational content on how to interact with the village's green technologies.

* **Visitor Guide:** Essential information on village ethics, "Eco-Rules," safety regulations, and "No-Plastic" zones.

* **Eco-Market:** Direct access to natural products with multi-currency support (AZN, USD, EUR).
* **Interactive Smart Map:** Real-time navigation to points of interest, eco-friendly transport, and smart amenities.
